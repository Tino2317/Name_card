About Me

This is who I am and what I do...

My full name is Tinodana Petros Mugwazo. I reside in Johannesburg, South Africa. I am a Full-Stack Python Developer and a Cisco Certified Network Associate (CCNA) pursuing a career in Network Engineering. I've created this portfolio to display some of my python skills and networking projects I've done.

My interest in networking and coding sparked during my University placement year at the Tshwane University of Technology. I became fascinated on how devices communicate with each other securely over the Internet and the automation, protocols, authentication and encryption methods behind it.

I then studied and acquired the CCNA certification to gain fundamental knowledge on how networking devices communicate with each other through routing and switching, and the different networking protocols implemented in order to have functioning, secure and efficient networks.

Simultaneously I also studied a beginner to advanced python course from Udemy. It focused on giving students real life projects to solve complex problems which make work and life so much easier through python scripting, automation, web development and designing, data science, APIs and web scraping.

My goal is to acquire as much knowledge and experience in network engineering and eventually go into Network Security, as I enjoy working in a team to solve complex problems, beneficial to the firm and clients.
